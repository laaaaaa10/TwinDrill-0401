
					The twindrill 0401

The twindrill 0401 project is a tiltrotor aircraft with 2 jet engines, and it is highly inspired by the virtual singer kasane teto. 
The name twindrill stands for the 2 jet engines which look like teto's drill like hair when they are mounted on the wings to provide power. 
The 0401 represents teto's creation date of April 1st, which serves as the official designation for the aircraft. Since it is a tiltrotor, the engines can rotate vertically, making the plane’s silhouette look exactly like teto's hairstyle whether it is taking off or flying.
